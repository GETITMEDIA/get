Here's a quick rundown of how it works:


Google Ads is an online advertising platform developed by Google. It allows businesses and individuals to create ads that appear on Google's search results pages, YouTube, and other sites within Google's Display Network.


Ad Creation

Users create ads and choose where they want them to appear. Ads can be text-based, display banners, video ads, etc.

Targeting

Advertisers select specific keywords, demographics, locations, and interests to target their ads to relevant audiences​

Bidding

Google Ads operates on a bidding system where advertisers bid on keywords or placements.

Ad Auction

When someone searches for a keyword or visits a site where your ad might appear, Google runs an auction to determine which ads are shown and in what order.

Performance Tracking

Advertisers can monitor the performance of their ads through Google Ads’ analytics tools, which provide data on impressions, clicks, conversions, and more. ​
